About Me

I'm a designer passionate about merging utility, function, and aesthetics in fashion. My work spans structured outerwear, technical garments, and experimental silhouettes.

With a background in design and hands-on experience in patternmaking, garment construction, and technical documentation, I enjoy crafting thoughtful pieces from initial sketches to finished garments.

This site is a curated portfolio of my recent projects and ongoing explorations.
